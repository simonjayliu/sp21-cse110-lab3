Venting Paragraph

I'm glad I woke up early again today, my sleep schedule has been getting much better. I've been waking up pretty late and not being able to fall asleep at night, which I found was a major issue. Thus, to try and resolve it, I would start waking up early no matter how late I slept so that the next night I would be tired enough to fall asleep quickly.

The absolute worst part about today was that while I was preparing for my interview at around 2pm, I get a phone call from New York. I pick it up and I hear a mechanical keyboard being clicked and then someone asks, "Is this Simon?" I anser yes, thinking this must be a scam phone call. However, I hear something about an interview. In the span of milliseconds everything clicks together. New York is eastern time, it's currently 2pm, and my scheduled time was 5pm. I suddenly realize that this is my interviewer and I apologize repeatedly. In the end, he said it's fine and we can reschedule for next week. Although it was a relief for me, what a horrible first impression on my part. I learned my lesson: always pay attention to the time zone for appointments in the professional world.
